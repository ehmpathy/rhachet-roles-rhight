# define.licence-qualifications.electrician.indiana.indianapolis

## .what

requirements to obtain an electrician licence in Indianapolis, Indiana (Marion County).

## .licence types

Indianapolis issues two electrician licence types:

| type | scope | prerequisite |
|------|-------|--------------|
| **residential** | one- or two-family homes | 6 years experience |
| **master (unrestricted)** | commercial and residential | residential licence first |

> "Indianapolis recognizes two license types for electricians: a Master (Unrestricted) and a Residential Electrician license."
>
> — [Indiana Contractor License Requirements by County](https://www.indianapermits.com/guides/indiana-contractor-license-requirements/)

## .experience requirements

| licence | experience required |
|---------|---------------------|
| residential | 6 years in electrical trade |
| master | residential licence + additional experience |

> "6 years of work experience or 4 years of work experience with relevant college courses"
>
> — [PHCEid: How to Become an Electrical Contractor in Indiana](https://www.phceid.org/how-to-become-an-electrical-contractor-in-indiana/)

**substitution allowed:** up to 2 years may be substituted with college courses, trade school, or apprenticeship programs.

## .application materials

| document | requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| exam application | completed form |
| experience letter | notarized letter from master electrician (must document 6 years) |
| customer references | 3 signed letters |
| driver's licence | photocopy |
| credentials | copies of diplomas, certificates, other licences |

> "A notarized letter from a master electrician [that documents] a whole 6 years of experience"
>
> — [PHCEid](https://www.phceid.org/how-to-become-an-electrical-contractor-in-indiana/) (paraphrased)

## .examination

| detail | residential | master |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| questions | 80-100 multiple choice | 100 multiple choice |
| duration | 3-4 hours | 4 hours |
| format | open book (NEC allowed) | open book (NEC allowed) |
| pass score | 75% | 75% |
| provider | Prometric | Prometric |

> "The exam requires a [score of] 75% [to pass]."
>
> — [ServiceTitan: Indiana Electrical License](https://www.servicetitan.com/licensing/electrician/indiana) (paraphrased)

**Prometric catalog numbers:**
- master: 20113 or 20115
- residential: 20313 or 20315

## .bond requirements

> "Indianapolis requires a $10,000 electrical contractor license bond."
>
> — [MG Surety Bonds: Indianapolis Contractor License Bonds](https://mgsuretybonds.com/indianapolis-contractor-license-bonds/)

| requirement | amount |
|-------------|--------|
| licence bond | $10,000 |
| typical annual premium | $100-$300 |

## .insurance requirements

| coverage | minimum |
|----------|---------|
| general liability | $500,000 (typical) |
| workers compensation | as required by Indiana law |

## .renewal

> "You need to renew the license once every two years after [you earn] it."
>
> — [PHCEid](https://www.phceid.org/how-to-become-an-electrical-contractor-in-indiana/) (paraphrased)

| detail | value |
|--------|-------|
| cycle | biennial (every 2 years) |
| expiration | December 31 of odd-numbered years |

## .process summary

| step | action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | accumulate 6 years experience (or 4 + education) |
| 2 | gather application materials |
| 3 | obtain $10,000 licence bond |
| 4 | obtain insurance |
| 5 | submit application to DBNS |
| 6 | pass Prometric exam (75%+) |
| 7 | attend orientation |
| 8 | receive licence |
