# define.licence-reciprocity.electrician.indiana.indianapolis

## .what

Indianapolis electrician licence reciprocity is **test-based**, not city-based. the Board of Electrical Examiners only reciprocates with jurisdictions that use specific Prometric exams.

**critical:** reciprocity does NOT mean automatic authorization. you must still **apply for a separate Indianapolis licence**. reciprocity only waives the exam requirement.

## .the rule

> "The Board may reciprocate a license from another area if the applicant can provide proof of current status of the license in that jurisdiction, with an original letter from the appropriate agency, that the applicant was tested with Prometric Inc. (formerly Thomson Prometric, Experior Assessments LLC, Block and Associates) Catalog No. 20113 or 20115 Master Electrical 4-hour exam or Catalog No. 20313 or 20315 Residential Electrical three-hour examination with a score of 75.0% or higher, and meets all other requirements."
>
> — [Indianapolis Board of Electrical Examiners, Electrical Reciprocal Information](https://media.graphassets.com/eAd7vuFgQYWAITnpLlLi)

## .required tests

| licence type | accepted exams |
|--------------|----------------|
| master | Prometric Catalog No. 20113 or 20115 (4-hour exam) |
| residential | Prometric Catalog No. 20313 or 20315 (3-hour exam) |

**minimum score:** 75.0%

## .not limited by state

> "There is not a limit on certain states for attaining a reciprocal license in Indianapolis, however they require not only proof of the [extant] license, but also an original document of the written exam for that license."
>
> — [US Licenses: Indiana Electrical License](https://uslicenses.com/indiana-electrical-license/) (paraphrased)

any jurisdiction worldwide can qualify — **if** they used the correct Prometric exam.

## .reciprocity process

| step | requirement |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | submit application packet to Board Coordinator (10+ business days before hearing) |
| 2 | provide proof of current licence status (original letter from issuer) |
| 3 | provide original exam document (must show Prometric + catalog number + score) |
| 4 | appear before Board of Electrical Examiners for interview |
| 5 | Board determines eligibility |
| 6 | if approved, attend orientation |

> "The applicant appears before the Board of Electrical Examiners for an interview, and the Board determines if the applicant meets the requirements for a reciprocal license."
>
> — [Indianapolis Board of Electrical Examiners](https://media.graphassets.com/eAd7vuFgQYWAITnpLlLi)

## .board schedule

> "The Board of Electrical Examiners meets the second Thursday of each month at 1:30pm."
>
> — [Indianapolis Board of Electrical Examiners](https://indianapolis.granicus.com/boards/w/f4a1ea9c05a8da69/boards/34356)

## .cities that do NOT qualify

jurisdictions that use non-Prometric exams cannot reciprocate. example:

| city | test provider | eligible? |
|------|---------------|-----------|
| Richmond, IN | Prov | **no** |

> "The city of Richmond accepts online examination results from Prov."
>
> — [City of Richmond: Contractor Licensing](https://www.richmondindiana.gov/resources/licensing)

Prov ≠ Prometric, so Richmond licences do not qualify for Indianapolis reciprocity.
