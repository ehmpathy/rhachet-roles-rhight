# define.licence-reciprocity.electrician.indiana.richmond

## .what

Richmond, Indiana accepts electrician licences from other jurisdictions **if** the applicant passed the same exam with 75%+ score.

**critical:** reciprocity does NOT mean automatic authorization. you must still **apply for a separate Richmond licence**, pay fees, and obtain a bond. reciprocity only waives the exam requirement.

## .the rule

> "If a private independent testing agency is utilized, a license shall be granted to any applicant who has previously taken and scored at least seventy-five percent (75%) on the same exam in another jurisdiction."
>
> — [City of Richmond: Contractor Licensing](https://www.richmondindiana.gov/resources/licensing)

## .test provider

Richmond uses **Prov** for examinations:

> "The city of Richmond accepts online examination results from Prov."
>
> — [City of Richmond: Contractor Licensing](https://www.richmondindiana.gov/resources/licensing)

therefore, reciprocity likely applies to jurisdictions that also use Prov exams.

## .reciprocity requirements

| requirement | detail |
|-------------|--------|
| exam provider | must be same as Richmond (Prov) |
| exam score | 75% or higher |
| application | complete Richmond licence application |
| fee | application fee required |
| bond | $5,000 licence bond required |
| verification | letter from prior jurisdiction may be required |

> "Such applicants must complete an application for a license, pay a fee, and provide a License Bond. It may be required to provide a letter of verification from the jurisdiction or institution where the applicant was previously examined."
>
> — [City of Richmond: Contractor Licensing](https://www.richmondindiana.gov/resources/licensing)

## .cities that do NOT qualify

jurisdictions that use different test providers cannot reciprocate:

| city | test provider | eligible for Richmond? |
|------|---------------|------------------------|
| Indianapolis | Prometric | **no** — different provider |
| Fort Wayne | varies | check with Richmond |

Prometric ≠ Prov, so Indianapolis licences do not qualify for Richmond reciprocity.

## .process

| step | action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | confirm your jurisdiction uses Prov exams |
| 2 | obtain letter of verification from prior jurisdiction |
| 3 | complete Richmond licence application |
| 4 | obtain $5,000 licence bond |
| 5 | pay application fee |
| 6 | submit to Richmond |
