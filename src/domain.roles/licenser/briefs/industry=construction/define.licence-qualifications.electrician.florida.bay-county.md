# define.licence-qualifications.electrician.florida.bay-county

## .what

requirements to obtain an electrician licence in Bay County, Florida (Panama City area).

## .florida's hybrid system

Florida uses a **hybrid** licence structure:

| level | authority | scope |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| electrical contractor | state (DBPR) | statewide, can pull permits |
| journeyman electrician | local (county) | work under contractor |
| master electrician | local (county) | supervision authority |

**key insight:** to work as an electrician in Bay County, you need a **local journeyman or master licence**. to run your own business and pull permits, you need a **state contractor licence**.

## .path to journeyman licence

### experience requirement

| requirement | detail |
|-------------|--------|
| apprenticeship hours | 8,000 hours (4 years full-time) |
| classroom hours | 576 total (144 hours/year × 4 years) |
| supervision | under licensed journeyman or master |

> "Applicants must complete at least 8,000 hours of electrical work experience under the supervision of a licensed electrician."
>
> — Florida DBPR, Contractor Licensure Requirements

### exam

| detail | value |
|--------|-------|
| exam type | journeyman electrician |
| pass score | 75% |
| format | open book |
| topics | NEC code, electrical theory, safety |
| provider | PSI or Prometric (varies by county) |

### fees

| fee type | amount |
|----------|--------|
| exam application | $50-$100 |
| exam fee | $100-$150 |
| licence fee | $50-$100/year |
| total initial | ~$200-$350 |

## .apprenticeship programs

### formal programs (recommended)

| provider | type | duration |
|----------|------|----------|
| IBEW Local 915 | union apprenticeship | 4-5 years |
| Gulf Coast State College | technical program | 2 years + OJT |
| ABC Gulf Coast | non-union apprenticeship | 4 years |

### informal path

work under a licensed electrician while self-study or attend night classes. slower but flexible.

## .total cost estimate

| category | range |
|----------|-------|
| apprentice wages | paid position |
| classroom/books | $500-$2,000 |
| exam fees | $150-$250 |
| licence fees | $50-$100 |
| tools (personal) | $500-$1,000 |
| **total out-of-pocket** | **$1,200-$3,350** |

**note:** apprentices earn wages while they learn. you are not paid to take classes, but you are paid for work hours.

## .timeline

| phase | duration | milestone |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| year 1 | 2,000 hours + 144 classroom | apprentice level 1 |
| year 2 | 2,000 hours + 144 classroom | apprentice level 2 |
| year 3 | 2,000 hours + 144 classroom | apprentice level 3 |
| year 4 | 2,000 hours + 144 classroom | exam eligible |
| exam | 1-2 months | journeyman licence |

**total: 4-5 years** from start to licensed journeyman.

## .insurance requirements

to work independently or as a contractor:

| coverage | minimum |
|----------|---------|
| general liability | $100,000 per occurrence |
| aggregate | $300,000 |
| property damage | $50,000 |
| workers comp | required if employees |

## .comparison: florida vs indiana

| aspect | florida (bay county) | indiana (indianapolis) |
|--------|---------------------|------------------------|
| apprenticeship | 8,000 hours required | varies by city |

| timeline | 4-5 years | 4+ years typical |
