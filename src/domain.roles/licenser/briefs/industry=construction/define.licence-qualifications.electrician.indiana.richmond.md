# define.licence-qualifications.electrician.indiana.richmond

## .what

requirements to obtain an electrician licence in Richmond, Indiana (Wayne County).

## .licence types

Richmond issues a **Master Electrician** licence.

> "Applicants should choose 'Self Registration,' with Certifying Body 'IN - City of Richmond' and Certificate type 'Master Electrician.'"
>
> — [City of Richmond: Contractor Licensing](https://www.richmondindiana.gov/resources/licensing)

## .examination

| detail | value |
|--------|-------|
| provider | Prov |
| registration | provexam.com/scheduler |
| certifying body | IN - City of Richmond |
| certificate type | Master Electrician |
| pass score | 75% |

> "The city of Richmond accepts online examination results from Prov."
>
> — [City of Richmond: Contractor Licensing](https://www.richmondindiana.gov/resources/licensing)

> "To qualify for a new license, an applicant must earn a score of not less than seventy-five percent (75%)."
>
> — [City of Richmond: Contractor Licensing](https://www.richmondindiana.gov/resources/licensing)

## .bond requirements

> "Each applicant to be granted an Electrical Contractor's License must also provide a License Bond, payable to the City in the penal sum of five-thousand dollars ($5,000.00)."
>
> — [City of Richmond: Contractor Licensing](https://www.richmondindiana.gov/resources/licensing)

| requirement | amount |
|-------------|--------|
| licence bond | $5,000 |

## .fees

| fee type | amount |
|----------|--------|
| exam application | $25 + independent test agency fee |
| annual licence | $100 |

> "Application fee for examination for a trade license: $25 + independent testing agency fee"
>
> "Annual City Trade License: $100"
>
> — [City of Richmond: Contractor Licensing](https://www.richmondindiana.gov/resources/licensing)

## .online account requirement

> "In order to apply for permits and schedule inspections online, contractors must have a BS&A account with the City of Richmond. Prior to online registration, all contractors must license or provide proof of license with the City."
>
> — [City of Richmond: Contractor Licensing](https://www.richmondindiana.gov/resources/licensing)

BS&A portal: bsaonline.com

## .process summary

| step | action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | register at provexam.com/scheduler |
| 2 | select "IN - City of Richmond" as certifying body |
| 3 | select "Master Electrician" as certificate type |
| 4 | pass exam with 75%+ score |
| 5 | obtain $5,000 licence bond |
| 6 | pay $25 application fee + test agency fee |
| 7 | pay $100 annual licence fee |
| 8 | receive licence |
| 9 | create BS&A account for permits |
