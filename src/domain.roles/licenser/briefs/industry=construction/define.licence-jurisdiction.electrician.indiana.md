# define.licence-jurisdiction.electrician.indiana

## .what

in Indiana, electrician licences are **local** — issued by cities and counties, not the state. a licence from one city is **not valid** in another.

## .the patchwork

Indiana delegates electrician licensure to municipalities. each city or county runs its own:
- examination
- licence issuance
- renewal
- enforcement

> "Indiana does not have a statewide regulatory board or a single statewide license for electricians. Instead, electrician licensure and the enforcement of codes are handled at the local level by individual municipalities, cities, and counties."
>
> — [Contractor Requirements: Indiana Electrician License](https://contractorrequirements.com/electrician/indiana/) (paraphrased)

## .state vs local in Indiana

| profession | authority | portal | one licence covers... |
|------------|-----------|--------|----------------------|
| plumber | Indiana PLA (state) | mylicense.in.gov | entire state |
| HVAC | Indiana PLA (state) | mylicense.in.gov | entire state |
| electrician | city/county (local) | varies by city | that city only |

## .why the difference

the 10th Amendment reserves police power (health, safety, welfare) to states. states can retain that power or delegate it to local governments.

Indiana retained control over plumbers and HVAC at the state level (via PLA). but Indiana delegated electrician licensure to cities and counties.

there's no single policy reason — it's historical. different professions organized differently, and Indiana ended up with a patchwork.

## .reciprocity is test-based, not automatic

a licence from City A does **not** automatically authorize work in City B. however, some cities offer **reciprocity** if the applicant's original exam meets their requirements.

| city | reciprocity basis | test provider required |
|------|-------------------|------------------------|
| Indianapolis | specific Prometric exams | Prometric (Catalog No. 20113, 20115, 20313, 20315) |
| Richmond | same exam as Richmond uses | Prov |

**key insight:** Indianapolis and Richmond use **different test providers** (Prometric vs Prov). therefore, a Richmond licence does NOT qualify for Indianapolis reciprocity, and vice versa.

> "The Board may reciprocate a license from another area if the applicant...was tested with Prometric Inc."
>
> — [Indianapolis Board of Electrical Examiners](https://media.graphassets.com/eAd7vuFgQYWAITnpLlLi)

> "If a private independent test agency is utilized, a license shall be granted to any applicant who has previously taken and scored at least seventy-five percent (75%) on the same exam in another jurisdiction."
>
> — [City of Richmond: Contractor Licensure](https://www.richmondindiana.gov/resources/licensing)

to work in a new city, check whether your extant exam qualifies for reciprocity — or prepare to take that city's exam.

## .reciprocity ≠ automatic authorization

**critical distinction:** reciprocity only waives the **exam**. you must still:

| requirement | with reciprocity | without reciprocity |
|-------------|------------------|---------------------|
| apply to new city | **yes** | yes |
| pay application fees | **yes** | yes |
| obtain licence bond | **yes** | yes |
| appear before board (if required) | **yes** | yes |
| take the exam | **no** (waived) | yes |
| receive **separate licence** | **yes** | yes |

> "Such applicants must complete an application for a license, pay a fee, and provide a License Bond."
>
> — [City of Richmond](https://www.richmondindiana.gov/resources/licensing)

> "the applicant appears before the Board of Electrical Examiners for an interview, and the Board determines if the applicant meets the requirements for a reciprocal license"
>
> — [Indianapolis Board of Electrical Examiners](https://media.graphassets.com/eAd7vuFgQYWAITnpLlLi)

**bottom line:** each city = separate licence. reciprocity saves you from a re-test, not from a new application.

## .contractors with multiple licences

some electricians hold licences in multiple cities. this is legal and common for contractors who serve a regional area.

**verification requirement:** if a contractor claims multiple licences, verify **each one separately** with the relevant city.

## .red flags

| situation | concern |
|-----------|---------|
| contractor shows licence from different city | not authorized to work in your city |
| "my licence covers the whole state" | false — no state electrician licence exists |
| "I don't need a licence here" | may be unlicensed or dishonest |
| refuses to show local licence | may not have one |

## .verification by city

since each city controls its own licences, verification methods vary:

| city | verification method |
|------|---------------------|
| Indianapolis | [Accela portal](https://aca-prod.accela.com/INDY/GeneralProperty/PropertyLookUp.aspx?isLicensee=Y&TabName=HOME) or (317) 327-8700 |
| Richmond | call (765) 983-7341 or email csittloh@richmondindiana.gov |
| Fort Wayne | contact local permit office |
| other cities | contact that city's permit/licence office |

## .the bottom line

**always verify the licence matches the work location.**

a valid Indiana electrician licence means:
- valid in the **issuer city only**
- must be **active** (not expired, suspended, or revoked)
- must match the **contractor's name**
