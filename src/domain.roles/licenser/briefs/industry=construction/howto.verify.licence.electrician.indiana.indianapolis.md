# howto.verify.licence.electrician.indiana.indianapolis

## .what

step-by-step process to verify an electrician licence in Indianapolis, Indiana (Marion County).

## .key fact: local licensure

Indianapolis electricians are **locally licensed**, not state licensed. the Board of Electrical Examiners under the Department of Business and Neighborhood Services (DBNS) controls electrician licensure in Indianapolis and Marion County.

> "The Board of Electrical Examiners carries out the provisions relative to licensure of electrical contractors in the city and county."
>
> — [Board of Electrical Examiners, Indianapolis](https://indianapolis.granicus.com/boards/w/f4a1ea9c05a8da69/boards/34356)

### step-by-step: online verification

| step | action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | go to [aca-prod.accela.com/INDY](https://aca-prod.accela.com/INDY/GeneralProperty/PropertyLookUp.aspx?isLicensee=Y&TabName=HOME) |
| 2 | select "Licensee" search type |
| 3 | enter contractor name, business name, or licence number |
| 4 | click search |
| 5 | review licence status, type, and expiration date |

## .information to gather first

> "When you check the license of an Electrician you will need [...] the contractor's full name, the contractor company's name, and the number that appears on the license."
>
> — [PHCEid](https://www.phceid.org/how-to-find-out-if-a-contractor-is-licensed-in-indiana/)

| required | where to obtain |
|----------|-----------------|
| contractor full name | ask contractor |
| company name | business card, invoice, or ask |
| licence number | ask contractor (should be on proposal) |

## .licence types in indianapolis

Indianapolis issues two electrician licence types:

| type | scope |
|------|-------|
| **master (unrestricted)** | commercial and residential work |
| **residential** | residential work only |

> "Indianapolis recognizes two license types for electricians: a Master (Unrestricted) and a Residential Electrician license."
>
> — [Indiana Contractor License Requirements by County](https://www.indianapermits.com/guides/indiana-contractor-license-requirements/)

## .what to verify

| field | green light | red flag |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| status | active | expired, suspended, revoked |
| expiration | future date | past date |
| type | matches your project | residential licence for commercial work |
| discipline | none | actions on record |

### renewal cycle

> "A licensed contractor whether it be Plumber, HVAC, or Electrician must renew their license every 2 years. Make sure their license is up to date!"
>
> — [PHCEid](https://www.phceid.org/how-to-find-out-if-a-contractor-is-licensed-in-indiana/)

## .additional verification steps

### request documentation directly

> "Qualified contractors are proud to be licensed and should be very open to share their license with you."
>
> — [PHCEid](https://www.phceid.org/how-to-find-out-if-a-contractor-is-licensed-in-indiana/) (paraphrased)

legitimate electricians will provide:
- licence certificate
- insurance certificate (general liability + workers comp)
- bond certificate

### cross-reference

verify the business name matches exactly across:
- licence documentation
- insurance documentation
- bond documentation
- proposal or contract

## .red flags at verification

| red flag | possible issue |
|----------|----------------|
| "I don't need a licence" | may be unlicensed |
| refuses to provide licence number | may be expired or revoked |
| licence from another city | not valid in Indianapolis |
| "just renewed" but portal shows expired | may be dishonest |
| no insurance or bond | financial risk |

## .common mistake: state vs local

do **not** use the Indiana PLA portal ([mylicense.in.gov](https://mylicense.in.gov/everification/)) for Indianapolis electricians. that portal is for **state-licensed** professions (plumbers, HVAC). Indianapolis electricians are **locally licensed** through DBNS.

| profession | licence authority | portal |
|------------|-------------------|--------|
| plumber | Indiana PLA (state) | mylicense.in.gov |
| HVAC | Indiana PLA (state) | mylicense.in.gov |
| electrician | Indianapolis DBNS (local) | aca-prod.accela.com/INDY |
