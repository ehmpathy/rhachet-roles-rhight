# ref.electrical-license-structure.indiana

## .what

Indiana has no statewide electrical contractor license. Each municipality sets its own requirements.

## .why this matters

- cannot assume state license transfers between cities
- must check local requirements for each jurisdiction
- Indianapolis has its own registration system via DBNS

## .indiana structure

| level | exists? | authority |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| state electrical license | NO | n/a |
| municipal electrical license | YES | each city/county |
| electrician certification | YES | local trade boards |

## .indianapolis electrician levels

Indianapolis recognizes three electrician levels:

| level | permit authority | supervision |
|-------|------------------|-------------|
| apprentice | cannot pull permits | requires supervision |
| journeyman | can perform work | limited permit authority |
| master electrician | can pull permits | can supervise, certify work |

## .contractor requirements

for contractors to perform electrical work in Indianapolis:

> "Any contractor must be or employ a master electrician."

contractors must also:
- register with Marion County DBNS
- maintain required insurance
- provide proof of surety bond

## .implications for permit research

when a user asks about permit requirements:

1. identify the specific municipality (not just state)
2. check local contractor registration requirements
3. verify homeowner exemption rules for that jurisdiction
4. do not assume reciprocity between Indiana cities
