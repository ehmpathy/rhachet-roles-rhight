# howto.relocate-tenant-during-major-repairs

## .what

how to handle tenant displacement during major repairs like K&T rewiring, roof replacement, or mold remediation.

## .why

> **voluntary repairs mean landlord pays. a month off rent often costs less than hotel logistics and builds goodwill.**

---

## who pays what

| expense | who pays | why |
|---------|----------|-----|
| repair work | landlord | voluntary capital improvement |
| tenant housing | landlord | unit uninhabitable due to landlord's choice |
| rent during displacement | tenant doesn't pay | rent abatement — can't charge for uninhabitable unit |
| lost rent | landlord absorbs | loss of rents coverage doesn't cover voluntary repairs |

---

## the "month off rent" approach

instead of booking hotels, offer tenant a full month rent-free for 1-2 weeks of displacement.

### why this works

| benefit | explanation |
|---------|-------------|
| often cheaper | month rent (~$1,500) vs hotel 2 weeks ($1,400-$3,500) |
| tenant flexibility | they choose where to stay — family, friends, vacation |
| vacation framing | turns disruption into opportunity |
| no logistics | no hotel bookings, no receipts to chase |
| buffer for overruns | if work takes 2.5 weeks, you're still covered |
| relationship building | tenant remembers generosity |

### cost comparison

| approach | typical cost |
|----------|--------------|
| hotel 2 weeks | $1,400 - $3,500 |
| airbnb 2 weeks | $1,200 - $2,800 |
| month off rent | ~$1,500 |

month off rent is often cost-neutral or cheaper, with better tenant relations.

---

## document the agreement

simple written agreement protects both parties:

```
TEMPORARY RELOCATION AGREEMENT

Tenant: [name]
Property: [address]
Landlord: [name]

1. Tenant agrees to vacate the unit from [start date] to [end date]
   to allow for [describe work: electrical rewiring, etc.].

2. Landlord waives rent for [month/period].

3. Tenant is responsible for their own accommodations during
   this period.

4. Landlord will notify tenant when the unit is ready for
   re-occupancy and provide at least [48 hours] notice.

5. If work extends beyond [end date], landlord will [describe
   accommodation: extend rent waiver, provide hotel, etc.].

Landlord signature: _________________ Date: _______
Tenant signature: _________________ Date: _______
```

## timeline expectations

| repair type | typical duration |
|-------------|------------------|
| K&T rewiring (full) | 1-4 weeks |
| panel upgrade only | 1-3 days |
| roof replacement | 3-7 days |
| mold remediation | 3-10 days |

factors that extend timeline:
- permit inspection delays
- hidden damage discovered
- contractor availability
- home size and accessibility

## indiana-specific considerations

indiana has no statutory requirement for landlord-paid relocation assistance during voluntary repairs. however:

| requirement | source |
|-------------|--------|
| must maintain habitability | IC 32-31-8-5 |
| cannot charge rent for uninhabitable unit | common law |
| tenant may terminate if landlord fails to accommodate | IC 32-31-7 |

**key insight**: while not legally required to pay for tenant housing, failing to accommodate can lead to lease termination and vacancy — which costs more than a month's rent.

---

## when hotel is better than rent credit

not every tenant can use a rent credit effectively:

| situation | better approach |
|-----------|-----------------|
| tenant has no family/friends nearby | book hotel/airbnb directly |
| tenant has limited funds | prepaid accommodation |
| tenant is elderly or disabled | arrange accessible lodging |
| tenant has pets | find pet-friendly option |
| very long displacement (4+ weeks) | hybrid: some hotel + rent reduction |

**always confirm** tenant has somewhere to go before assuming rent credit works.

---

## total cost math

for a typical K&T rewiring with 2-week displacement:

| item | cost range |
|------|------------|
| full K&T rewiring | $12,000 - $25,000 |
| tenant accommodation (month off rent) | ~$1,500 |
| lost rent (already covered above) | $0 additional |
| **total project cost** | **$13,500 - $26,500** |

compare to force-placed insurance at 8x premiums ($9,600/year) — rewiring pays for itself in 2-3 years of premium savings.

---

## communication timeline

| when | action |
|------|--------|
| 60+ days before | inform tenant of planned work |
| 30 days before | confirm dates, discuss accommodation |
| 14 days before | sign written agreement |
| 3 days before | final confirmation, key handoff if needed |
| during work | progress updates every few days |
| work complete | 48-hour notice before re-occupancy |
| after return | walk-through to confirm unit condition |
