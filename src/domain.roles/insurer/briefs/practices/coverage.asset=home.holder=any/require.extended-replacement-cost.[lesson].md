# require.extended-replacement-cost

## .what

extended replacement cost (ERC) endorsement adds 25-50% buffer above policy limit for $25-50/year.

## .why

> **$25-50/year buys 25% buffer against $139,000 average underinsurance gap. this is baseline, not optional.**

---

## the math

| scenario | coverage | rebuild cost | gap |
|----------|----------|--------------|-----|
| no ERC | $400,000 | $500,000 | $100,000 |
| with ERC (25%) | $500,000 | $500,000 | $0 |
| **annual cost** | **$25-50** | | |

ERC costs ~$3/month. average underinsurance gap is $139,000.

**ROI**: $50/year × 30 years = $1,500 lifetime cost vs $139,000 potential gap.

## coverage tiers

| tier | buffer | availability | cost |
|------|--------|--------------|------|
| standard (no endorsement) | 0% | default | included |
| extended replacement cost | 10-50% (typical 25%) | most carriers | $25-50/year |
| guaranteed replacement cost | unlimited | limited carriers, newer homes | varies |

## when ERC kicks in

ERC only applies when:
1. **total loss** or near-total loss occurs
2. **rebuild costs exceed** policy limit
3. policyholder **actually rebuilds** (not cash settlement)

does NOT apply to:
- partial losses
- cash-out settlements
- contents/personal property (separate endorsement needed)

---

## ERC vs guaranteed replacement cost

| feature | ERC | GRC |
|---------|-----|-----|
| buffer | capped (25-50%) | unlimited |
| availability | most carriers | limited |
| home age restrictions | few | often excludes older homes |
| cost | $25-50/year | significantly higher |
| post-disaster protection | partial | complete |

---

## why ERC matters more now

### construction cost volatility

| period | cost increase |
|--------|---------------|
| 2020-2022 | 30-40% |
| 2024-2025 | 5.2% |
| post-disaster surge | 50-100% |

inflation guard (2-4%/year) cannot keep pace with surge.

### algorithm underestimation

360Value (used by 2/3 of market) systematically underestimates rebuild costs:
- foundation data wrong 50% of time
- custom features missed
- local labor costs outdated

ERC provides buffer against systematic underestimation.

---

## how to add ERC

1. **call insurer or agent** — request "extended replacement cost endorsement"
2. **confirm buffer percentage** — 25% is standard, some offer 50%
3. **verify it applies to structure** — not just contents
4. **check annual cost** — should be $25-50/year range
5. **get confirmation in policy** — endorsement number varies by carrier

typical endorsement codes:
- ISO: HO 04 90 (contents RCV, different from ERC)
- varies by carrier for structure ERC

## state regulations

### colorado (regulation 5-1-25, july 2024)

- insurers must offer ERC of at least **50%** (up from 20%)
- law and ordinance coverage of at least **20%** (up from 10%)
- response to Marshall Fire underinsurance findings

### california (SB 876, proposed 2025-2026)

- require **minimum 50% ERC** on all residential policies
- require carriers to **offer GRC option**
- automatic 50% increase if home lost in declared emergency
- double penalties for fair claims violations in emergencies

### california (CCR 10-2695.183)

- replacement cost estimates must include labor, materials, supplies
- licensees must annually verify sources/methods are current

### texas (bulletin B-0022-20)

- disclosure required when RCV changed to ACV
- carriers must report roof coverage basis (effective April 2026)

### oregon (ORS 742.270)

- policies may not limit or deny ERC payment for total loss structures

## post-disaster evidence

### marshall fire (2021) — the key study

| metric | value |
|--------|-------|
| homes destroyed | 1,084 |
| claimants with ERC | 87% |
| claimants underinsured | 74% |
| ERC holders still underinsured | 57% |
| average shortfall | $139,000 |

**result**: each 10% increase in underinsurance reduces rebuild permit likelihood by 4% within one year. (Philadelphia Fed, 2025)

### california wildfires (2017-2018)

- tubbs fire: two-thirds underinsured, some gaps exceeded $1M
- camp fire: construction costs doubled post-fire
- 360Value estimates typically 30-50% below actual rebuild costs

### LA fires (2025)

- 75% of total-loss claimants underinsured
- average replacement cost: $955K (Palisades) vs $574K (Eaton)

**conclusion**: ERC is necessary but not sufficient. combine with annual coverage review and independent appraisal.
