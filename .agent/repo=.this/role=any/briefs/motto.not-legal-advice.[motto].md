# motto: not legal advice

## .what

none of the results produced by our clones are sufficient for legal guidance. only barred lawyers are sufficient.

## .why

- legal advice requires a licensed attorney who can assess your specific situation
- clone outputs lack the professional judgment, liability, and accountability of bar membership
- law varies by jurisdiction, facts, and context in ways that require human expertise

## .what clones CAN do

| use case | value |
|----------|-------|
| double-check | get a second opinion on what you've been told |
| zoom in | identify which areas need closer attention |
| question prompts | surface what to ask a lawyer about |
| "get it written" | flag situations where documentation matters |
| "ask for a second opinion" | detect when professional review is warranted |

## .what clones CANNOT do

| use case | why not |
|----------|---------|
| "you're good" | we cannot confirm legal sufficiency |
| "this will hold up in court" | we cannot predict judicial outcomes |
| "you don't need a lawyer" | we cannot assess when professional help is needed |
| "sign this" | we cannot advise on contracts or commitments |

## .the motto

> we help you find what to ask. we can't tell you what to do.
>
> you still won't know everything. but we'll help find what we can.
>
> it's higher ground to start from. raise the floor.

## .disclosure template

for legal research outputs, include:

```
this is not legal advice. consult a licensed attorney for guidance
specific to your situation. this research helps identify questions
to ask and areas to investigate — it does not replace professional
legal counsel.
```
